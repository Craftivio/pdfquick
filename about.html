About PDFQuick

Free PDF tools for everyone, everywhere

Our mission: Make PDF tools free, fast, and private for everyone — no signup, no uploads, no limits.

What is PDFQuick?

PDFQuick is a free online PDF toolkit that lets you merge, compress, split, protect, watermark, rotate PDFs and convert between PDF and images — all directly in your browser. No files are ever uploaded to our servers. Everything happens locally on your device.

Why We Built PDFQuick

Most PDF tools online either charge money, require signups, upload your files to servers, or show you annoying ads before you can do anything. We built PDFQuick to fix all of that. Every tool is free, instant, and completely private.

Our 8 Free PDF Tools

  • 🔗 Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDFs into one
  • 🗜️ Compress PDF — Reduce file size without losing quality
  • ✂️ Split PDF — Extract pages or split into separate files
  • 🔒 Protect PDF — Add password protection
  • 🖼️ PDF to Image — Convert pages to JPG or PNG
  • 📸 Image to PDF — Convert images to PDF
  • 💧 Watermark PDF — Add custom text watermarks
  • 🔄 Rotate PDF — Rotate pages by any angle

How It Works

PDFQuick uses modern browser technology (JavaScript, WebAssembly) to process your PDF files entirely on your device. This means your files never leave your computer — they are never uploaded to any server, never stored anywhere, and never seen by anyone except you.

Who Uses PDFQuick?

PDFQuick is used by students, professionals, teachers, lawyers, doctors, and everyday people who need to work with PDF files quickly and privately. Our users come from over 50 countries worldwide.

Is PDFQuick Really Free?

Yes — completely free. No credit card. No subscription. No hidden fees. Core PDF tools will always be free on PDFQuick. We are supported by non-intrusive advertising.
