About PDFQuick

Free PDF tools for everyone, everywhere

Our mission: Make PDF tools completely free, fast, and 100% private for everyone — no signup, no uploads to servers, no limits.

Our Story

PDFQuick was created because most PDF tools online are frustrating to use. They charge money, require account signups, upload your sensitive files to unknown servers, or show endless ads before you can do anything. We thought there had to be a better way.

So we built PDFQuick — a collection of 8 essential PDF tools that work entirely in your web browser. Your files never leave your device, you don't need to create an account, and everything is completely free to use.

What is PDFQuick?

PDFQuick is a free online PDF toolkit that lets you merge, compress, split, protect, watermark, rotate PDFs, and convert between PDF and image formats. All processing happens directly in your browser using modern web technologies like JavaScript and WebAssembly.

Our 8 Free PDF Tools

We currently offer the following free PDF tools, all working entirely in your browser:

  • Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDFs into one document
  • Compress PDF — Reduce PDF file size with quality options
  • Split PDF — Extract pages or split into separate files
  • Protect PDF — Add password protection to PDFs
  • PDF to Image — Convert PDF pages to JPG or PNG
  • Image to PDF — Convert images to PDF documents
  • Watermark PDF — Add custom text watermarks
  • Rotate PDF — Rotate pages by any angle

How It Works

Unlike most PDF tools that upload your files to remote servers for processing, PDFQuick uses modern browser technology to handle everything locally on your device. This means:

  • Your files are never uploaded to our servers
  • No one can access your private documents
  • Processing is instant — no waiting for uploads
  • Works offline once the page is loaded
  • No file size limits beyond your device memory

Who Uses PDFQuick?

PDFQuick is used by students, professionals, teachers, lawyers, doctors, freelancers, and everyday people who need to work with PDF files quickly and privately. Our users come from over 50 countries worldwide including the United States, United Kingdom, India, Canada, France, Germany, and many more.

Why We Built PDFQuick

The team behind PDFQuick noticed three major problems with existing online PDF tools:

  1. Privacy concerns — Most services upload your files to their servers
  2. Hidden costs — Free trials that turn into expensive subscriptions
  3. Annoying limitations — Daily usage limits, signup requirements, watermarks

We built PDFQuick to solve all of these problems at once. Free, private, no signup, no limits.

Is PDFQuick Really Free?

Yes — completely free. No credit card required. No subscription. No hidden fees. All 8 PDF tools will always remain free to use. We support our development costs through non-intrusive advertising on the website.

Our Technology Stack

PDFQuick is built with modern web technologies for maximum performance and reliability:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • PDF Processing: pdf-lib and PDF.js libraries
  • Authentication: Supabase
  • Hosting: Vercel global CDN
  • Security: HTTPS encryption everywhere
