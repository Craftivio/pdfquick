Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Short version: Your files never leave your browser. We don't upload, store, or sell your PDFs. Ever.

1. Who We Are

PDFQuick provides browser-based PDF tools at usepdfquick.com. We help you merge, compress, split, and protect PDF files online for free.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account data: Email and encrypted password if you create an account
  • File metadata: File name and size only (never content)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT upload your PDF files to any server
  • We do NOT read or access file content
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT store your files anywhere

4. How Files Are Processed

All PDF processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Files are loaded into your browser's memory, processed locally, and made available for download — without ever touching our servers.

5. Cookies & Advertising

We use essential cookies for login sessions. We display non-intrusive ads from Google AdSense to support our free service. AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (auth/database), Vercel (hosting), Google AdSense (ads), and Google Analytics (anonymous statistics).

7. Your Rights

You can access, delete, or export your data anytime. Contact us through the website.

8. Children's Privacy

PDFQuick is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check the "Last updated" date for changes.

10. Contact Us

Questions? Visit usepdfquick.com to get in touch.
